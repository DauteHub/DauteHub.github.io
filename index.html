Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the National Research University "Moscow Power Engineering Institute" (MPEI). My work and academic interests focus on Materials Science, concentrated energy processing technologies, and the design of equipment and tooling for advanced material processing applications. Most of my experience has been in laboratory research and industrial projects involving laser-cladded components, material characterisation, and the application of destructive and non-destructive testing methods to evaluate material integrity and performance.

I have also worked on projects in collaboration with Plakart JSC, focusing on the development of wear-resistant solutions for drilling equipment through laser cladding technologies and surface engineering approaches.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy writing poetry and short stories, reading philosophy and history, playing football, and running. You can learn more about my projects, research activities, achievements, and other interests throughout the different sections of this portfolio.

Feel free to reach out with any questions.
