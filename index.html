Thank you for visiting my portfolio!

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the National Research University "Moscow Power Engineering Institute" (MPEI). My work and academic interests focus on Machines and Technologies for High-Efficiency Material Processing using Concentrated Energy Flows (CEF), Materials Science, and the design of specialized equipment and tooling for advanced material processing applications.

Most of my experience has been in laboratory materials research and industrial projects involving laser and electron beam technologies, as well as surface engineering approaches.

I have also worked on projects in collaboration with Plakart JSC, focusing on the development of wear-resistant solutions for drilling equipment through laser cladding technologies.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy writing poetry and short stories, reading philosophy and history, playing football, and running. Through this portfolio, you can explore my projects, research activities, achievements, and other interests across the different sections.

Feel free to reach out with any questions.
